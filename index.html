About Us

Why You Should Trust Us? Get To Know About Us!

Mushrad is a start-up company under Technology Transfer, Licensing and Commercialisation Centre which is dedicated to producing a superior ultrasound gel that revolutionizes healthcare diagnostics and treatments. Our gel is designed to be a game-changer in various medical applications, including diagnostic ultrasound scanning, physiotherapy treatments, Doppler assessments, and fetal heart rate monitoring in antenatal clinics. With a strong focus on innovation, Mushrad aims to deliver an acoustically perfect and high-quality ultrasound gel, setting a new standard in the industry and driving growth across all aspects of our business.

Why Choose Us

Mushrad Ultrasound Sono Gel is the only locally produced ultrasound gel, ensuring unmatched freshness and reliability. Its acoustically perfect formula delivers crystal-clear images, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Certified and compliant with SAZ regulations, Mushrad guarantees safety and effectiveness.

Mushrad sono gel is customizable to specific requirements and leaves no residual effect, ensuring patient comfort. Plus, the more you buy, the cheaper it becomes, offering unbeatable value.
